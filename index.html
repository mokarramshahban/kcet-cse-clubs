Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about technical clubs at KCET? Here are some quick answers to help you understand how to get involved and make the most of your journey.

Technical clubs at KCET are student-led communities focused on coding, innovation, research, and collaboration. They organize workshops, hackathons, and skill-based events.

Students can join clubs through open registrations conducted every semester. Keep an eye on department notices or club announcements to participate.

Clubs organize coding competitions, seminars, industrial visits, project expos, mentorship programs, and hands-on training sessions in trending technologies.

Any student from the CSE department with an interest in technology, innovation, or learning is welcome to join and actively participate in club activities.

Participating in clubs builds technical skills, boosts confidence, encourages networking, and enhances your resume—giving you a strong edge in placements and future opportunities.

KCET CSE Clubs foster innovation, coding, and collaboration among students to build a better technological tomorrow.
