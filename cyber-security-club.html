Empowering the Next Generation of Cyber Defenders

The Cyber Security Club is a dynamic community of students passionate about ethical hacking, digital safety, and emerging security technologies. Our mission is to promote awareness, hands-on learning, and skill development in the ever-evolving field of cyber security. Through workshops, capture-the-flag events, expert talks, and real-world simulations, we strive to prepare our members for the challenges of modern digital threats.

Technologies We Use in Cyber Security Club

Our club engages with a wide range of cutting-edge tools and technologies across multiple domains of cybersecurity, allowing members to gain hands-on experience and practical exposure to real-world scenarios.

Network Security

Tools: Wireshark, Nmap, Cisco Packet Tracer

Ethical Hacking

Tools: Kali Linux, Metasploit, Burp Suite

Cryptography

Tools: OpenSSL, Hashcat, RSA/AES

Web App Security

Tools: SQLMap, KSSer, OWASP ZAP

Malware Analysis

Tools: Autopsy, Volatility, FTK Imager

Cloud Security & SIEM

Tools: Splunk, ELK Stack, Wazuh

Club Activities

The Cyber Security Club organizes a wide range of engaging and skill-enhancing activities aimed at building real-world experience and fostering a strong cybersecurity mindset among members.

Red vs Blue Team Drills

Simulated attack and defense scenarios to test and improve incident response and strategy.

Capture The Flag (CTF)

Competitive cybersecurity challenges to test skills in real-time threat solving and exploitation.

Workshops & Hands-on Labs

Guided sessions on tools, techniques, and industry practices to build hands-on capabilities.

Seminars with Experts

Learn directly from professionals working in cybersecurity, for career guidance and trend updates.

Bug Bounty Projects

Explore ethical vulnerability reporting and collaborate on real-world bug bounty programs.

Secure Codeathons & Hackathons

Participate in development-focused events with an emphasis on building secure software solutions.
